Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is the largest city in Nigeria and Africa and is one of the fastest-growing urban areas in the world, with over 21 million population. It is known for its vibrant music scene, featuring genres like Afrobeats and Juju, and has a rich cultural identity. Lagos is also home to the continent's busiest port, handles 80% of Nigeria's imports, and was the former capital of Nigeria until 1991.
